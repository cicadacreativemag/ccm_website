YOU ARE HERE

island  
paradise—  
*deep inside*  
*the red corallium reefs*  
*first taste of scuba dive*


YOU ARE HERE

*a cloud*  
*of swooping*  
*swallows—*  
snapdragons   
sprout 


YOU ARE HERE

*frigid air*  
*in the porch*  
the loyal collie  
wags at its  
master's whistles
