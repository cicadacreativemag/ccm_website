Organic&nbsp;&nbsp;Into the days of wanting night —  
we stick our heads  
in pillowcases.&nbsp;&nbsp;The hardwood floors have lost their varnish  
and have paint dots spattered  
on their faces.  
So we step lightly —  
knowing violence  
&nbsp;&nbsp;in lacking our own place.
