Karlando Butt
@kxrlymiyagi
Karlando Butt (Kxrly Miyagi) is a self-taught pixel artist from Mandeville, Jamaica, inspired by his love for video games and their storytelling potential. For over seven years, he has honed his craft, blending classic game influences with original creations that capture Jamaican culture and everyday life. His work finds beauty in the mundane, using minimal yet evocative pixel art to highlight overlooked moments and underrepresented perspectives. With a passion for visibility and storytelling, he aims to carve out space for culture within the pixel art world.

Forrest Russell
@4rest.russell
Forrest Russell is a multidisciplinary artist based out of Montreal, Quebec. He has obtained both a Diploma of pre-illustration and animation from Algonquin College (2012-2013) as well as a Bachelor of Fine Arts Majoring in Studio Arts from Concordia University (2018-2023). Russell is interested in exploring various ways of logging movement. Many of his works capture this through the accumulation, collaging and archiving of objects and images. These collected timestamps act as evidence of our everyday habitual patterns that we as a society often overlook. This process is a meticulous and time-consuming curation. How these pathways intertwine and connect is at the core of Russell’s research aiding him in tracing the trajectory of the world around him.
